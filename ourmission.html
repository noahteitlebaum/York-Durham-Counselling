Our Mission

Welcome to York Durham Counselling, where:

“Compassionate client care meets expert therapy.”

York Durham Counselling was created upon recognizing the barriers within mental health services, including long wait lists for service, insufficient resources, and limited effectiveness. We offer a level of expertise and experience that sets us apart from less experienced or newly trained therapists.

All of the therapists at York Durham Counselling are driven by a commitment to deliver the highest quality of therapy, ensuring that every client receives the care they need and deserve.

Why Choose York Durham Counselling?

Expertise

All therapists at York Durham Counselling have a minimum of 20 years of direct clinical experience and remain current with advancements in their field.

Compassion

Each therapist approaches every client with empathy and understanding, creating a safe, supportive environment for healing and growth.

Client-Centered Approach

Therapy is tailored to each individual’s needs, prioritizing personalized care that supports meaningful and lasting change.

Online Sessions

Flexible online counselling provides accessible, stress-reduced care from home, eliminating travel time and offering scheduling convenience.
