Welcome to York Durham Counselling

"Compassionate Client Care Meets Expert Therapy"

Why choose York Durham Counselling?

  • Expertise: All of the therapists that work for York Durham Counselling have a minimum of 20 years direct clinical experience and remain up to date on advancements in their field.
  • Compassion: All of the therapists at York Durham Counselling approach each client with empathy and understanding, creating a safe space for healing and growth.
  • Client-Centered: York Durham Counselling is committed to tailoring therapy to meet the unique needs of each client; they prioritize personalized care leading to lasting solutions.
  • Online Sessions: York Durham Counselling provides online counselling sessions to improve the ability for clients to access mental health support from home, eliminating travel time and stress. This also allows York Durham Counselling to offer flexible scheduling to fit various lifestyles.
